Why use it?

Choosing a university is not any decision!

We seek to help pre-university students select the best university, for this, we use 5 indicators mainly:

  • Installation and infrastructure
  • Teachers
  • Mobility (how accessible is it to get there)
  • Extracurricular activities
  • Scholarships and supports

How do we do it?

With the help of people like you and me.

The College Friend application seeks to create an active community of people who share information and experiences in the form of texts and images so that students can have elements that serve as support to select the university that best suits their expectations and needs.

With the help of AI and high security standards, we maintain the integrity of the information of all users and the content they share.
